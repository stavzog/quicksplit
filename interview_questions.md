# Interview Script

## Introduction 2mins

"I'm working on a project for my CS class about how people manage shared costs. I’m interested in your real experiences—no right or wrong answers here."

1. "Can you tell me about the last time you went out with a group where the bill had to be split?" 

## Exploring the Process 8-10 mins

2. "Walk me through what happened when the bill arrived. Who took charge? How was the math handled?" 

3. "What tools did you use to track those costs (e.g., Splitwise, Venmo request, a physical receipt)? Why did you choose that specific method?"


4. "Have you ever decided not to use an app because it required everyone to make an account or download something? Tell me about that specific situation." 

## Identifying Pain Points 8-10 mins

5. The "Awkwardness" Factor: "Tell me about a time when settling up felt awkward or stressful. What made it feel that way?" 

6. Data Integrity: "Have you ever looked at a group total and felt like the math was wrong, or that a transaction was missing? How did you handle it?"


7. Long-term Tracking: "When you share recurring costs (like utilities or a long trip), how do you keep track of who is 'ahead' or 'behind' over time?" 

## Closing Surprising Takeaways 2-3 mins
8. "If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about how your friend group handles money, what would it be?" 

9. "Is there anything else about sharing expenses that we haven't talked about yet?"
